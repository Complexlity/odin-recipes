Fried Yam

Description

One of the utility foods that can be used as both appetizer, snacks and main meal

Ingredients

  • Yam tubers
  • Onions
  • Groundnut Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Slice the yam tuber into bits (depending on the usage smaller slices for snacks and larger slices for main meal) and peel back off
  2. Sautee the Onions and wash
  3. Put the oil in frying pant and put on cooker until the oil boils
  4. Add the onions the the boiled oil and leave for a few minutes
  5. Add the slices until the maximum limit of the pan + oil is reached
  6. Leave for a few minutes and use turner to turn the yam faces upside down`
  7. Leave for another few minutes and remove the yam
  8. Repeat steps 5 - 7 until all the yam have been fried
  9. Serve with stew and tea for better experience
