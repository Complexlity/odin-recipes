Jollof Rice

Description

This is a West African stew made with rice, chilli peppers, and meat or fish.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomato paste
  • Pepper (fresh and grounded)
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Maggi
  • Oil (Palm or Groundnut)
  • Other Spices (optional)
  • Water

Steps

  1. Parboil the rice for 30 minutes
  2. Remove from the heat and wash the rice with clean water to remove the dirt
  3. Rinse the pot used to parboil the rice to remove all present rice particles
  4. Put some oil in the pot then the pot on the cooker and allow the oil to heat
